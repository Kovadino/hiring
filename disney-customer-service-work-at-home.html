About the Company

Disney is one of the world’s most renowned entertainment companies, offering an unparalleled blend of innovation, creativity, and storytelling through its diverse range of products and services. Known for its timeless characters and unforgettable stories, Disney values its fans and believes in providing magical experiences to its customers in every endeavor.


About the Job


At Disney, we are passionate about creating the best possible experiences for our customers. We believe in teamwork, collaboration, and continuous learning, providing our employees with the tools and resources they need to achieve success both personally and professionally.

As a Disney employee, you will have the opportunity to work from home and join our dynamic team, where you’ll play a crucial role in delivering exceptional customer experiences. In this role, you’ll work as a remote customer service representative, answering inquiries from customers regarding all things Disney.


Responsibilities:

In this role, your primary responsibilities will include:

  • Answering customer inquiries relating to Disney products and services
  • Solving customer issues using excellent problem-solving skills
  • Maintaining a high level of customer satisfaction
  • Supporting team members and contributing to a positive work environment

Qualifications:

Disney is looking for someone who possesses the following qualifications:

  • A self-starter who thrives in a remote work environment
  • Able to exercise good judgment, think critically, and use resources effectively to resolve customer inquiries and issues
  • Strong listening and oral communication skills
  • The ability to work in a fast-paced environment, multitask, and prioritize assignments

Requirements:

  • A high-speed internet connection
  • A quiet work environment
  • A personal computer running Windows 10 Home or Pro, MAC OS 10.13 or higher with at least 4 GB RAM, i5 processor or better, and a solid-state hard drive with at least 256 GB of available space.
  • Ability to complete initial home-office set-up without reimbursement
  • Availability to work flexible hours including mornings, evenings, weekends, and holidays.

Benefits and Compensation:

Disney offers a competitive salary and benefits package, including medical and dental insurance, 401(k), and paid time off. As an employee, you’ll also have access to generous discounts on Disney products and services.
