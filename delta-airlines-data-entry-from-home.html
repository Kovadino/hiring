About Us:

Delta Airlines is a leading global airline committed to providing exceptional travel experiences to our customers. We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Data Entry Specialist to join our team on a remote basis. As a Data Entry Specialist, you will play a crucial role in maintaining and updating our databases with accuracy and efficiency.

Responsibilities:

  • Enter and update customer and flight-related data into our database systems.
  • Verify accuracy and completeness of data entries to ensure data integrity.
  • Assist in reconciling discrepancies and errors in data.
  • Generate and maintain reports as required.
  • Collaborate with team members to ensure timely and accurate data processing.

Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or equivalent.
  • Proven experience in data entry or related administrative roles.
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Proficient computer skills, including MS Office and data entry software.
  • Excellent organizational and time management abilities.
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information.
  • Effective communication skills, both written and verbal.

Requirements:

  • Flexible work schedule.
